About the Add-on

The "Localization for App Store Connect App Version by OLIVA" Google Sheets add-on helps developers efficiently translate and update their iOS app descriptions in multiple languages using Google Translate and Apple‚Äôs App Store Connect API.

This tool reads app metadata from your spreadsheet, sends it to Google Translate for localization, then pushes updates to App Store Connect. At the end of the process, it sends a summary email listing all apps retrieved and those successfully updated.

How We Use Your Data

This add-on accesses the following data only as needed to complete its functionality:

  • Reads spreadsheet content to extract app metadata
  • Calls external APIs (Apple & Google Translate) to perform updates
  • Sends an email report to your Google Account address via MailApp

Your data is never stored or shared outside of these purposes. Email addresses are used solely to send execution results to the current user.
